About

An artist who lives and works in Atlanta Georgia. While she makes her living in the film industry, she understands the inherent philosophical difference between photography and cinematography, as reflected in her work. Her current ambition is to understand the overlap between an art and a craft, while her life’s ambition is to master four arts, one of which is photography. Her retirement plan is to get a master’s degree in Library Science.

While she believes in the value of receiving a good education, crafting has existed for many years outside of the university college system. She actively seeks out various masters to learn from and will continue to hone her various crafts. Michelle is very much in touch with her heritage, allowing for her unique perspective to been seen through her work.
